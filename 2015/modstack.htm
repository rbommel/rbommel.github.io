Outline

See program below. We will treat the algebraic stack \(\mathcal{M}_g\), look at the definition, properties, some applications and maybe its compactification.

Program

  • Categories fibered in groupoids, descent, \(\mathcal{M}_g\) as a stack
  • \(\mathcal{M}_g\) as algebraic stack
  • Deformation theory, relative dimension of \(\mathcal{M}_g\) over \(\mathrm{Spec}(\mathbb{Z})\), smoothness of \(\mathcal{M}_g\)

We will also aim to treat at least one (but hopefully multiple) of the following topics.

  • Applications of stack theory
  • Compactification of \(\mathcal{M}_g\)
